PUM Mission Statement

PUM is a community gathering place for locals and foreigners alike to experience and celebrate the diverse cultures and unique blend of nationalities that make the melting-pot which is the Pacific Northwest so great. A meeting place for artisans, farmers, businesses both foreign and domestic, as well as nonprofit organizations and charities to connect with the public and share their passions with the community, whether it be with quality foods, drinks, goods, or services.


About us

Welcome to Seattle’s premier international market. PUM opened for business in 2018 and is driven by the hard-working, passionate vendors and local businesses who share in their passion to showcase their fresh made, high quality products directly connecting producers to end-users showing us why everything from foods to drinks and goods to games are best served straight from the source. Come join our world class market and eat, learn, laugh, and grow with us into 2019 and beyond.

Seattle

According to an American Community Survey, people who speak Asian languages at home account for 10% of the population, followed by Spanish at 4.5%. Seattle's foreign-born population has increased 40% in ten years. Of the Asian population, 4.1% are Chinese with origins in mainland China, Hong Kong and Taiwan. Seattle also has a large Vietnamese community with more than 30,000 Somali immigrants. The Seattle metro area is also home to one of the largest Samoan populations in the mainland United States. (2010)
